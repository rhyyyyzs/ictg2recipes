Bicol Express

Bicol Express is a famous Filipino dish known for its rich, creamy, and spicy flavor. It’s made with pork simmered in coconut milk, chili peppers, shrimp paste, and aromatics. The dish originated from the Bicol region, which is known for its love of spicy food and coconut-based dishes.

bicol express
  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 30-40 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Meat: 1/2 kg Pork belly (cut into small cubes)
  • Liquid: 1 1/2 cups Coconut milk (gata)
  • Aromatics: 1 whole head of Garlic (minced), 1 Onion (chopped), 1 thumb-sized Ginger (sliced)
  • Flavoring: 2–3 tbsp Shrimp paste (bagoong alamang))
  • Spice: 5–10 pcs Red chili peppers (adjust depending on spice level)
  • Optional: 1 cup Coconut cream (for thicker sauce), a bit of oil for sautéing

Instructions:

  1. Sauté: Heat oil in a pan. Sauté garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Cook Pork: Add pork and cook until it lightly browns and releases oil.
  3. Add Bagoong: Stir in the shrimp paste and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Simmer: Pour in coconut milk. Cover and simmer on medium heat until pork becomes tender (about 25–30 minutes).
  5. Add Chili: Put the chili peppers and continue cooking.
  6. Thicken: Add coconut cream (optional) and simmer until the sauce becomes thick and creamy.
  7. Adjust Taste: You can add a little salt or sugar depending on your preference.
