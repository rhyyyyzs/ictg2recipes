Ginataang Bangus

Ang Ginataang Bangus ay isang tradisyunal na Filipino dish na gawa sa bangus (milkfish) na niluto sa gata ng niyog. Kilala ito sa Visayas at Bicol regions, at perfect sa mainit na kanin at family meals.

Ginataang Bangus
  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 lbs bangus boneless
  • 8 grams Maggi Magic Sarap
  • 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 8 pieces string beans cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 3 pieces Thai chili pepper
  • 1 piece onion, minced
  • 1 thumb ginger, minced
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. Season the bangus with Maggi Magic Sarap.
  2. Dredge the fish in all-purpose flour, making sure it is evenly coated. Set aside.
  3. Heat the cooking oil in a pan. Fry the bangus until both sides turn light to medium brown. Remove and set aside.
  4. Using 2 tablespoons of the oil from frying the fish, stir-fry the string beans for about 90 seconds. Remove and set aside.
