Sarciadong Isda

Sarciadong isda is a Filipino dish of fish cooked in a sauce made with tomatoes, onions, and eggs. The fish is typically fried or sautéed before being simmered in the sauce until cooked. “Sarciado” is a Filipino culinary term that refers to a cooking method where meat or fish is cooked with a thick sauce made primarily of tomatoes and eggs. The sauce is usually flavored with onions, garlic, and spices, giving it a savory and tangy taste.

Sarciadong Isda
  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 30-40 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 1 lb Pampano
  • 1 cup Chopped Roma Tomato
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Onions
  • 3 cups water
  • 3 large Beaten Eggs

Instructions:

  1. Clean the Tilapia or Pampano and remove the guts-season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the oil and fry the fish until cooked through. Remove the fish from the pan.
  3. Using the same oil for frying, saute the onion and the tomatoes. Cook until soft.
  4. Add the water and let it simmer to soften the tomatoes and onion. Add the fish sauce and season with salt and pepper. Season according to your liking.
  5. Beat the egg and pour into the simmering broth. Use a fork to make egg strips. Add the fried fish and simmer for three more minutes on low heat.
